# West Outlaw 🌵🤠

![Unity](https://img.shields.io/badge/Unity-2022.3+-black?logo=unity)
![Status](https://img.shields.io/badge/Status-Completed-success)
![Genre](https://img.shields.io/badge/Genre-Third--Person%20Shooter-red)

**West Outlaw** is a stylized cartoon third-person shooter set in an irreverent and action-packed Wild West. Developed as a **Final Graduation Project (TCC)**, the game blends fast-paced combat, strategic gameplay, and a unique visual identity inspired by industry-leading titles.

---

## 🎮 Game Modes

### 🚩 Online Multiplayer: Capture the Flag
Engage in competitive team-based matches (Red vs. Blue).
* **Objective:** Capture the flag located at the center of the map and return it safely to your team's base.
* **Victory:** The team with the most captures by the end of the match is declared the winner.
* **Dynamics:** Success requires a mix of agility, shooting precision, and team coordination to dominate the battlefield.

### 💀 Survival Arena (Offline / Singleplayer)
A pure endurance challenge for those who prefer a solo experience.
* **Objective:** Survive as long as possible against endless waves of outlaws.
* **Progression:** Each wave increases in difficulty, pushing your combat reflexes and resource management to the limit.

---

## ✨ Key Features

* **Visual Aesthetic:** A unique "stylized cartoon" look, drawing inspiration from *Crash Bandicoot 4*, *Fortnite*, and *Sea of Thieves*.
* **Diverse Maps:** Explore thematic environments including *Abandoned Town*, *Train Wreck*, and *Yard*.
* **Playable Characters:** Choose between Lane Cowboy and Annie Hardin.
* **Classic Inspirations:** A tribute to Western cinema and titles like *Red Dead Redemption*, combined with the frenetic action of *Serious Sam* and *Halo Infinite*.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Overview

* **Engine:** Built with Unity Engine.
* **Multiplayer:** Server-based networked multiplayer for stable online matches.
* **Platform:** PC (Windows).
* **Development:** Currently under constant development as part of a college project.
